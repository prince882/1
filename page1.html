Interactive Timeline of India's Independence

1857

The First War of Indian Independence, also known as the Sepoy Mutiny or Rebellion of 1857, begins.

1885

The Indian National Congress is founded, marking the beginning of an organized Indian nationalist movement.

1919

The Jallianwala Bagh Massacre occurs, intensifying Indian demands for independence.

1930

The Dandi March, led by Mahatma Gandhi, becomes a pivotal event in the Civil Disobedience Movement.

1942

The Quit India Movement is launched, demanding an end to British rule in India.

1947

India gains independence from British rule on August 15th.
